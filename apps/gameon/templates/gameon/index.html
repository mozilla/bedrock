Game on II

Build the next generation of browser based games

  • Re-imagine the web as a new game platform.
  • Make browser based 2D games, 3D games, mobile games, hackable games, open source games and games for learning.
  • Invent novel game mechanics, create new storylines, engage diverse audiences, introduce visually challenging content.
  • Win a trip to Game Developers Conference to meet industry pioneers.
  • And feature your game at Mozilla's Marketplace.

Submit a Game

Mozilla invites you to imagine how next-generation web technologies can revolutionize the way we make and play games. It's your opportunity to invent new game mechanics, create new storylines, engage diverse audiences, introduce aesthetically challenging content and re-imagine the web as the new game platform.

Submissions open on the 3rd of December 2012 and close on the 20th of February 2013.

Come to a Game Jam

We are hosting some game jams and you should come! If you have a game for the competition and need help testing and finishing it up, a technology you want to showcase or teach others how to use, ideas for new games or just want to come play with great new technologies and meet people who love making and hacking games as much as you do, join us at one of the following:

What? When? Where?
Gamercamp 3rd & 4th of November Toronto, Canada
Mozilla Festival's Game Arcade 10th & 11th of November London, United Kingdom

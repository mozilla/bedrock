Introduction

This page contains the visual identity for promoting Firefox OS. Because certain assets are subject to partnership and licensing agreements, we created these materials specifically and specially for community and developer use. They’re intended to represent the creation phase of Firefox OS and how all Mozillians play a part in bringing it to life.

Guidelines

For all permitted uses of these assets, you may not:

  • alter the poses in any way or create new ones
  • change the color of the white pencil-sketch line art or fill in the fox images with any color
  • alter the blue backgrounds provided or place the images on any other background
  • place these materials in such close proximity to other content that they are indistinguishable
  • make these materials the most distinctive or prominent feature on your website, printed material or other content
  • use these materials in a way that suggests any type of association or partnership with Mozilla or approval, sponsorship or endorsement by Mozilla (unless allowed via a license from us)
  • use these materials in a way that is harmful, deceptive, obscene or otherwise objectionable to the average person
  • use these materials on websites or other places containing content associated with hate speech, pornography, gambling or illegal activities
  • use these materials to, or in connection with, content that disparages us or sullies our reputation

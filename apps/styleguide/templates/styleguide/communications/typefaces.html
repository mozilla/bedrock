Our Wordmark System

Meta has been a Mozilla font since time immemorial — or at least since the early days of the project. Today it is only used in our tiered branding system, as outlined below.

Meta Bold

The old, trusted Mozilla standard, Meta Bold is reserved for our Mozilla wordmark and usage at the Foundation level. It’s our rock.

Meta Medium

At the product level, Meta Medium is our champion and creates a second tier of Meta font usage. Sleek, svelte, nimble.

Meta Normal

Our third tier of font usage features Meta Normal as the light and lean choice for sub-branding. Light in weight, heavy in awesome.

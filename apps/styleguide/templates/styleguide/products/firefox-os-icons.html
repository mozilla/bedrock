Basics

A Firefox OS application icon is a 60x60 pixel image provided in 24-bit PNG format. Each of the standard OS application icons is contained within or underlayed with a 58 pixel radius circle shape.

A drop shadow is automatically generated based on the icon’s silhouette shape; do not include a drop shadow in the image you provide.

Common silhouette shapes & recommended sizing

Please follow the suggested sizing for common shapes below.

If your icon has a unique silhouette, it is important that you gauge the weight of it in relation to the standard set of Firefox OS icons for maximum consistency. A PSD template has been provided for you to drop your icon in to test for proper sizing. See: MozillaFXOSIconGrid.PSD

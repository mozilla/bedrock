Partnership Opportunities

Mozilla has developed a range of partnerships with some of the world’s leading search, commerce, content, technology and service providers. We continue to partner with new organizations and look forward to working with you.

Mozilla offers many ways to partner including:

  • Mozilla Firefox – opportunities include distribution, customized versions, and more for our PC, Mac and Linux browser products. Learn more about Mozilla Firefox distribution policies.
  • Mozilla Firefox Mobile – opportunities include distribution, promotions, customized versions and more.
  • Firefox Apps Marketplace – Combine the popularity of an app with the distribution of the Web. Mozilla is seeking app publishers and developers to distribute apps across our desktop and mobile platforms, with a starting audience of 450M people!
  • Mozilla Boot2Gecko – this is Mozilla’s new web based device platform. If you are a mobile device and tablet OEM’s/ODM’s, or a mobile operator, then we want to talk to you.
  • Mozilla Persona – Connect with Persona, the safest & easiest way to sign in into the Web. Persona is a federated login that provides the highest degree of user privacy and security. If you would like to integrate Persona on your website, please visit mozilla.org/persona
  • Promotion or Marketing partnerships – Mozilla is always looking for marketing and promotion partnerships to drive user growth and loyalty. Reach out if there is a way to delight each other’s users.
  • Something else – If there is another partnership model or really cool idea you would like to discuss – contact us.

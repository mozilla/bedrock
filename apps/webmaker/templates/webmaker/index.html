Mozilla Webmaker

We're building a generation of webmakers — moving millions of people from being users of the web to makers of the web. Our mission is all about empowering users, and what better way is there to do that than to help them shape the web itself?

Join the Summer Code Party

This summer, we're inviting everyone to join us to meet up, make something cool, and learn how the code behind the web works. It's called the Summer Code Party, and it starts June 23.

Mozilla is joining with dozens of other organizations to make this happen. Want to make a prettier template for your blog? Or level up your latest YouTube video? Or just learn a bit of HTML? We'll have tools, tutorials, and activites to help you become a webmaker. Join the party here. [link]

Tools for learning

We're creating web-based apps that you can use to discover, remix, and create your own content online. Here's just a few that you can try today:

  • Hackasaurus: Activate the X-ray Goggles and instantly reveal and remix any page on the web. Share your hacks with your friends.
  • Popcorn: Bring the web into online video. Launch the Popcorn Maker web app (beta), select your favourite video, and you can pull in tweets, maps, photos, and more — all in realtime.
  • Webmaker (coming soon): Build a webpage from scratch, or try a pre-built template. See the code as you work, and learn as you make.

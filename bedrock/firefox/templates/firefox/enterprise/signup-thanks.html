Thank you for signing up for Firefox Browser for Enterprise.

Getting Started

Here is where you'll find documentation and support regarding how to configure and deploy Firefox Browser in your organization. You may choose to deploy the standard Firefox Rapid Release (RR), which receives performance and feature improvements every six weeks, or the Extended Support Release (ESR), which receives one major update per year. Both Firefox RR and ESR receive critical security fixes as soon as possible.

You can now configure Firefox via Group Policy — download ADMX templates here, or implement using a JSON flle.

For your convenience, we’ve also sent you an email with links to this documentation.

Give us your feedback

We would love to hear from you regarding any issues you might encounter in your testing, or if you have feature requests. To do so, we encourage you to complete this survey here.

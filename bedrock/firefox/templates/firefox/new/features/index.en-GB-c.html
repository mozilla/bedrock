Private Browsing mode

Protect your privacy and be free of annoying ads that follow you around the web. Private Browsing mode erases all passwords, third-party cookies and browsing history every time you use the Firefox browser.

{{ high_res_img('firefox/new/features/en/control-center.png', {'alt': '', 'width': '480', 'height': '401'}) }}

Enhanced Tracking Protection

Stop ads and websites from following, profiling and profiting off you. The Firefox browser gives you three levels of protection from third-party trackers, so you can decide what privacy means to you.

{{ high_res_img('firefox/new/features/en/sync.png', {'alt': '', 'width': '480', 'height': '360'}) }}

Seamless device sync

Get more control on your devices with a Firefox Account. Connect your desktop computer and mobile to send open tabs, sync bookmarks, safety access your passwords, save notes, and much more.

{{ high_res_img('firefox/new/features/en/password-manager.png', {'alt': '', 'width': '480', 'height': '343'}) }}

Smart password management

Access your passwords from anywhere. Lockbox remembers all of the passwords you save in the Firefox browser and keeps them safe. It syncs across devices, so you can save a password on desktop and pull it up later on your mobile.

{{ high_res_img('firefox/new/features/en/faster.png', {'alt': '', 'width': '480', 'height': '319'}) }}

Faster than ever

Take a ride on the fastest Firefox yet. This version of the Firefox browser is 2x faster than before and uses 30% less memory than Chrome. Stream videos without slowing down, and load tabs and websites at lightning speed.

{{ high_res_img('firefox/new/features/en/switch.png', {'alt': '', 'width': '480', 'height': '257'}) }}

Stress-free switching

Switch to the Firefox browser without losing your favorite bookmarks and settings. In a few minutes, Firefox imports your bookmarks, autofills, passwords, and settings from Chrome or any other browser.

Meet Firefox Quantum. Fast for good.

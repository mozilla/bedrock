Thanks for signing up

We appreciate your willingness to participate in research for our anti-harassment products. Your feedback will help us ensure we build products responsibly, and with your needs at the center.

Whats next?

Before we can email you about research, we need to make sure you’ve opted in to receiving emails from Mozilla. Unless you’ve done so before, please check your inbox for an email from us to click and confirm your subscription.

Once we find a study for you to participate in, our small research team will reach out to you to schedule a survey, a 1-on-1 interview, or a session to view live prototypes.

You can reach out to us directly at digitalsafetyresearch@mozilla.com with any questions or concerns.

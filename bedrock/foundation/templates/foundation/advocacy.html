{{ _('How we work: A focus on depth and scale') }}

{% trans %} Mozilla’s advocacy approach incorporates both scale to depth and depth to scale to properly address the diversity of issues that we work on. {% endtrans %}

{% trans %} This approach enables Mozilla to simultaneously build our capacity to mobilize millions of people quickly and grow our organizing power in local communities, which will make it possible to win on our issues in the long-term. {% endtrans %}

{% trans %} We also push ourselves to be proactive—setting the topic and tone of the conversation, and promoting solutions—as well as reactive to current events or undesirable policies when necessary. {% endtrans %}

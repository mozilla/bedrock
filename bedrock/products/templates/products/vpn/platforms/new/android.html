How does a VPN protect my Android device?

VPNs help prevent:

  • Your internet activity from being viewable, and then sold or shared with advertisers.
  • Low video streaming quality due to slower and restricted bandwidth from your internet service provider (ISP)
  • Your personal data being stolen when using a public wi-fi network.

Why should I download Mozilla VPN for Android?

  • Browse the internet without giving away your location.
  • Be more comfortable using public wi-fi in cafes, airports, and more.
  • Avoid intrusive location-based advertising.

Web And Services Bug Bounty Hall of Fame

On behalf of the Mozilla and the millions of people who visit our sites, use Firefox and our other products we would like to thank them for their hard work in helping to make us more secure.

As of this date, we have paid out over $4,000,000 across all of our bounties. Congratulations to everybody who has participated!

If your name is on the list incorrectly or you feel you should be on the list please feel free to mail us at security@mozilla.org.

Our Web bug bounty program has been migrated to HackerOne. Therefore, we will discontinue adding researchers on this page. Starting from May 2023, researchers will be recognized on our HackerOne pages, Mozilla Critical Services and Mozilla Core Services
